### In-Front Pipboy not Visible
Changing game Pipboy from "on-wrist" or "projected" to "in-front" will cause the Pipboy to be invisible.  
To use "in-front" Pipboy set it in game config and then restart the game.

### Two handed weapon movement doesn't align with primary hand
This is caused by the weapon "center" not aligned with primary hand grip location.
Apart from fixing the nif of the weapon it's not fixable at the moment.
